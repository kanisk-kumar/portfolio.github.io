about us
Welcome to ARIHANT INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL, NAHAN

The Arihant international School is one of the premier residential cum day boarding schools at Nahan Distt. Sirmour. It is a co-education English medium school. The school is situated 4kms away from Nahan town, is situated in God’s gifted natural and peaceful environment. Nahan is known for its picturesque surroundings and marvelous views of the Himalayan Mountains. The school is registered under the Management of Mata Padmawati Educational Society, Nahan. The society under the dynamic leadership of Sh.Anil Jain, Chairman & Mr. Sachin Jain, General Secretary The main purpose to join this school is to provide the education to the students with an ideal environment to develop their intellectual and creative energy. In view of the above, Management has started regular 6th to 10th, 10+1 & 10+2 science (Medical / Non-Medical stream,Arts & Commerce) affiliated to H.P Board, Dharmasala. This school offers excellent quality of education and focuses primarily on the all-round development of the child.
